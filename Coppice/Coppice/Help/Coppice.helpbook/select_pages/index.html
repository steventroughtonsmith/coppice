Select Pages

Pages can be selected on a Canvas. They can then be moved and removed from the Canvas. Selected Pages always show their title bar and have a highlight ring around them

Select a single Pages

To select a single page, simply click anywhere the page in the Canvas.

To add another page to the selection, or to remove an existing page from the selection, hold the shift key while clicking
Select multiple Pages
  1. Click on the background of the Canvas
  2. Drag the mouse over the pages you wish to select, a selection box will appear
  3. Release the mouse once you have selected pages
To add multiple pages to an existing selection, hold the shift key while dragging the mouse.
Select a branch

To select a Page and all child Pages branching off it:

  1. Hover over the Page at the root of the branch you wish to select
  2. Double click anywhere on the title bar except for the title itself (or the close button)
