Rotating an Image

From Coppice 2022.1, images in image pages can be rotated from inside the app.

Rotate an image 90° to the left
  1. Start editing the image page
  2. Click on the Rotate Left button {[image|rotating_an_image_left.jpg|style="width:33px"]}
Rotate an image 90° to the right
  1. Start editing the image page
  2. Click on the Rotate Right button {[image|rotating_an_image_right.jpg|style="width:33px"]}
