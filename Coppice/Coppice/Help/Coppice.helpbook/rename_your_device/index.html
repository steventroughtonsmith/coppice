Rename Your Device

As you can activate multiple Macs on a single Coppice Pro subscription, it's helpful to give them descriptive names to tell them apart when managing your subscription. By default, Coppice uses your computer's public name (what appears when sharing on a network or via Air Drop). However, you can always change this to something more descriptive if you wish:

Rename device
  1. Select Coppice > Preferences… (⌘,) from the menu bar
  2. Click on the Coppice Pro tab
  3. Hover over the device name.
  4. Click the Edit button
  5. Type the new name for your device in the text field
  6. Hit enter
