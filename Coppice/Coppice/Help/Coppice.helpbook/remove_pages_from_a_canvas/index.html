Remove Pages From a Canvas

Pages can be removed from a Canvas by closing them. Closing a Page will close it and any linked child branches spanning from that Page.

Closing a Page (and its child branches)
  1. {[link|select_pages|Select the Page(s)]} you wish to close
  2. Select Canvas > Close Selected Pages from the menu bar
    Alternatively, click the close button in the titlebar of a page

You should never be afraid to close child Pages and branches on a Canvas. As long as the root Page stays on the Canvas, Coppice will remember the size and position of every child branch, so the next time you click a link to open a page, all the pages will be restored.
