Activate a Device

Once you have purchased a Coppice Pro subscription, you can activate up to 3 Macs on it.

Activate a Mac
  1. Select Coppice > Preferences… (⌘,) from the menu bar
  2. Click on the Coppice Pro tab
  3. Enter your M Cubed Account email into the Email field
  4. Enter your M Cubed Account password into the Password field
  5. Click Activate Device

If you ever want to remove a device from your subscription, you can always {[link|deactivate_a_device|deactivate]}
