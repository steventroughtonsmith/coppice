Re-order the Canvas List

The Canvas List can be re-arranged, letting you keep your most important Canvases close at hand

Move a Canvas
  1. Select Canvases from the sidebar
  2. Click and drag a Canvas, an indicator will appear between canvases
  3. When the indicator is where you want the Canvas to be, release the mouse button
Canvases can only be re-arranged with a Coppice Pro subscription
