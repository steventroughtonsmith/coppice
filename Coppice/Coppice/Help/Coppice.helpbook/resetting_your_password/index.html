Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your M Cubed Account password, you can reset it online:

Reset your password
  1. Go to https://mcubedsw.com/forgot_password in your browser
  2. Enter the email address for your M Cubed Account
  3. Click Send Email
  4. Check your email inbox for a message title "M Cubed Account - Forgotten Password Reset"
  5. Click on the link in the email
  6. Enter your new password in the Password field
  7. Enter your new password again in the Confirm Password field
  8. Click Reset Password
If you don't receive the email, be sure to check your spam folder. If you are still having issues contact M Cubed Support
