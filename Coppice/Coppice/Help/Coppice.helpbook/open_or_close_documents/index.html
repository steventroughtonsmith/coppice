Open or Close Documents

Coppice can open documents of the "Coppice Document" format (which have the file extension .coppicedoc).

Open a Document
  1. Open Coppice by clicking on Coppice in the Dock, Launchpad, or Applications folder.
  2. Click on the Open… button in in the Welcome to Coppice window
    Alternatively, select File > Open… (⌘O) from the menu bar
  3. Select the document, then click Open
Open a Recent Document
  1. Open Coppice by clicking on Coppice in the Dock, Launchpad, or Applications folder.
  2. Find the recently opened document on the left of the Welcome to Coppice window and double-click it
    Alternatively, select the recently opened document from File > Open Recent in the menu bar
