Change the Zoom Level of a Canvas

Canvases can grow quite large, so it is useful to be able to zoom out and get a higher level view.

Change the zoom level

You can change the zoom level of a Canvas in multiple ways:

  • From the menu bar:
    1. Select Canvas > Zoom from the menu bar
    2. Choose whether to Zoom In (⌘>), Zoom Out (⌘<), or zoom to the Actual Size (⌘0)
  • From the canvas editor:
    1. Click on the zoom control in the bottom right of the Canvas editor
    2. Choose a zoom level
  • Using a trackpad, simply pinch to zoom in and out
