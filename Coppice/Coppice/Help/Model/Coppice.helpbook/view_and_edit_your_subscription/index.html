View and Edit Your Subscription

From time to time you may find you want to view and edit your Coppice Pro subscription. This can be done online in your M Cubed Account.

View your subscription
  1. Go to https://mcubedsw.com/account in your browser
  2. Enter your M Cubed Account email into the Email field
  3. Enter your M Cubed Account password into the Password field
  4. Click Log In
  5. Find the subscription you wish to view and click Manage Subscription

Once you have your subscription open you can {[link|deactivate_a_device|deactivate devices]}, updating billing info, and cancel your subscription
