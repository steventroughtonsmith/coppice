Customise the Toolbar

Coppice documents have a Toolbar at the top of the window, containing common actions such as creating a Page or Canvas, adding a Link to a page, or searching your document.

Show or Hide the Toolbar
  1. Select View > Hide Toolbar (⌥⌘T) from the menu bar to hide the toolbar
  2. Select View > Show Toolbar (⌥⌘T) from the menu bar to display the toolbar again
Customise the Toolbar
  1. Select View > Customise Toolbar… from the menu bar
  2. To add items to the toolbar, drag them from the sheet that appears to the toolbar
  3. To remove items from the toolbar, drag them out of the toolbar and release the mouse button
  4. To re-arrange the toolbar, drag an item to the location you want it to appear
On macOS Big Sur and higher some items cannot be moved (such as the Toggle Sidebar toolbar item)
